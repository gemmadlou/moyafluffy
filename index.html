Their Food

We have certainly discovered that even though they are brother and sister, their pallettes are different. There are things they both like, dislike, but they do have their differences.

They Both Eat...

  • Tin Cat Food - they're pretty impartial, but love fish
  • They love mackarel, cooked salmon and sardines
  • Cat nip - but it's an unusual like - they go crazy over it
  • They nibble on biscuits during the day until dinner time
  • Roast chicken pieces

Fluffy Loves...

  • Kettle chips - crisps in general, but loves ready salted or salt and vinegar
  • Peas and carrots, strangely enough
  • Olive oil...I don't know why

Moya Loves...

  • Chicken slices
  • Cheese - we don't give it often, but she likes a little piece

Their Chilltime

Apart from a few hours in the morning and evening when they're ready to play, they like to chill. On my bed, on the sofa, in front of the fire, watching cartoons if they're on, bird watching on the balcony. They enjoy the chilled life.
